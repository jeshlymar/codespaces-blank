Our Styling Services

Silk Press
Description
A smooth, shiny, and voluminous silk press designed to enhance natural hair without harsh chemicals. Perfect for special occasions or a sleek everyday look. A silk press is a temporary straightening method that uses heat to achieve a smooth finish while maintaining hair health. It is a method often used by individuals with natural curly or coily hair textures who want to experience straight hair without chemical relaxers. The silk press can last from a few days to a couple of weeks, depending on hair care and environmental factors.

Curly Cut
Description
A dry curl-by-curl shaping method that enhances your natural texture and improves curl structure. This technique is tailored to each individual's unique curl pattern, ensuring a personalized and flattering cut that promotes healthy hair growth and reduces frizz. The curly cut is performed on dry hair to accurately assess the natural fall and shape of the curls, allowing for precise cutting that enhances the hair's natural beauty. This service is ideal for those looking to embrace their natural curls and achieve a more defined and manageable hairstyle.

Protective Styling
Description
Braids, twists, sew-ins, and other long-lasting protective styles to promote growth and reduce damage. These styles shield natural hair from environmental stressors and manipulation, allowing for healthier hair over time. Protective styling involves creating hairstyles that minimize hair exposure to daily wear and tear, reducing the risk of breakage and split ends. These styles can last several weeks with proper care, making them a convenient option for those looking to maintain healthy hair while enjoying versatile looks.
