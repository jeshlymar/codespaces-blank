Defined Natural Curls Tutorial

Step 1: Section hair into manageable parts.
Step 2: Dampen hair.
Step 3: Apply leave-in conditioner.
Step 4: Detangle using a wide tooth comb.
Step 5: Apply styling gel .
Step 6: Use the shingling method to define curls.
Step 7: Ergo detangling brush.
Step 8: Scrunch curls upward to enhance definition.
Step 9: Diffuse or air dry.

• Use a microfiber towel to prevent frizz.
• Avoid touching curls while drying.
• Use products with strong hold for long-lasting definition.

Protective Style: Twists

Step 1: Apply Leave in cream.
Step 2: Section your hair.
Step 3: Apply oils to soothe and moisturize.
Step 4: Brush sections to evenly distribute product.
Step 5: Apply gel.
Step 6: Divide section into two strands.
Step 7: Twist strands around each other from root to tip.
Step 8: Secure ends with a small elastic band if needed.
Step 9: Repeat until all hair is twisted.

• Smaller twists = longer lasting style.
• Keep scalp moisturized with light oils.
• Do not overuse heavy products to avoid buildup.
