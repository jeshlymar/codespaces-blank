Defined Natural Curls Tutorial

Step 1: Start with damp hair.
Step 2: Apply leave-in conditioner.
Step 3: Detangle using a wide tooth comb.
Step 4: Apply styling gel using the raking method.
Step 5: Scrunch curls upward to enhance definition.
Step 6: Diffuse or air dry.

• Use a microfiber towel to prevent frizz.
• Avoid touching curls while drying.
• Use products with strong hold for long-lasting definition.

Protective Style: Twists

Step 1: Part hair into sections.
Step 2: Apply twisting cream.
Step 3: Twist tightly from root to end.
Step 4: Seal ends with oil.
Step 5: Let air dry or set under hooded dryer.

• Smaller twists = longer lasting style.
• Keep scalp moisturized with light oils.
• Do not overuse heavy products to avoid buildup.
